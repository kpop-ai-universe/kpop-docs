# How to make an AI Cover with Applio Colab

This guide will walk you through the process of creating AI covers using Applio on Google Colab.

---

## Step 1: Accessing Colab
This is Applio collab: 
[!ref target="blank" text="Open Applio Colab"](https://colab.research.google.com/github/iahispano/applio/blob/master/assets/Applio.ipynb#scrollTo=-7cQtXouqpQi)

## Step 2: Installation
Run the **"Install Applio"** cell.

![Install Applio](https://files.catbox.moe/jkw4k8.png)

## Step 3: Starting the App
After the 1st cell is done running, run the **"Start Applio"** cell and wait until you see a public url pop up. 

!!!info
Click on the public url to access the Applio gradio.
!!!

![Start Applio](https://files.catbox.moe/qe2y5m.png)

## Step 4: Download Voice Model
Once you're on the Applio gradio, click on the **"Download"** tab and copy the link of the voice model you want to use.

![Download Tab](https://files.catbox.moe/bte6mo.png)

## Step 5: Model Installation
Paste the link onto the **"Model Link"** box and press on **"Download Model"**. 

!!!success
Wait until the Output Information says "Model downloaded successfully".
!!!

![Download Process](https://files.catbox.moe/6jhjbx.png)

## Step 6: Inference Setup
Go to the **"Inference"** tab and click **"Refresh"** to have access to the voice model's `.pth` and index file.

![Inference Tab](https://files.catbox.moe/xb1msx.png)

## Step 7: Conversion
Upload your acapella file and press on **"Convert"** after the acapella file is done loading.

![Conversion](https://files.catbox.moe/q6qvrn.png)

## Step 8: Final Output
After the conversion is complete, you can download the output. 

!!!tip
You can mix this output with the instrumental using any audio editing software.
!!!

![Download Output](https://files.catbox.moe/29vt6z.png)
